Welcome to a random blog!

Here I will sometimes blog about some games that I have been playing. I will be analyzing their mechanics and overall game feel as well as commentating on whether or not I find the game fun at all. I will try and not talk about their respective fandom/community as that is another topic entirely as it pertains to more about internet culture then it does about game design or programming.

This blog will be slit into three main categoies: Mobile Gaming, Video Gaming, and Mechanics. In the Mobile Gaming and Video Gaming categories I will sometimes talk about some of the games that I have been playing on that respective medium and give my thoughts on them. The Mechanics category will cater to talking about the different mechanics in a certain genre and will be giving my thought on them.
